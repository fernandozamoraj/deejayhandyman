ABOUT DEEJAY HANDYMAN

Born in Indiana and raised in Southern California. I started DJing at the age of 15. My first venues were house parties and small dances. Like many around my block, I joined a local rap group; in which we performed at the Riverside Legion Hall.

After graduating high school, I entered the U.S. Army. Once in, I was able to afford professional equipment that all the top DJs used (Technics 1200s). While in the military, I DJ'ed at the local club on base. After a few years of moving from base to base, the bulk of my military DJing was in Dexheim Germany; under contract with the U.S. Military (Yep, they allowed me to moonlight on the side).

After leaving the military, I took a hiatus from DJing because of the cumbersome albums that needed to be transported. Fast forward to today's era… Digital music!!! I've been spinning since 2010 utilizing the NS7 by Numark; which pretty much negates totting albums. Welcome .mp3 format!!!

My largest event to date was the MMA event (which held 2000 people) at the Killeen Civic and Conference Center. And I've been spinning ever since!
